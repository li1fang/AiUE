# Community Workflow

## Collaboration Path

Preferred order:

1. Discussion for platform or stable-surface ideas
2. Issue for concrete bugs, regressions, or workflow-pack proposals
3. PR once scope and lane are clear

## Routing

- stable CLI/schema/policy/guard discussions go to `Core`
- workflow-pack behavior goes to `Workflow`
- experimental probe/capture work goes to `Labs`
- CI and repeatability work goes to `Reliability`

## Workflow-Pack Proposals

New workflow packs are encouraged.

Proposals should explain:

- the Unreal problem being solved
- why it belongs in AiUE
- what stable surface, if any, it needs
- whether it starts as a workflow or a lab
