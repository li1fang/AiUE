# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

We want `AiUE` to be a place where people can collaborate safely, argue clearly, and improve the project without hostility.

Expected behavior:

- be respectful and direct
- critique ideas, not people
- assume good intent, but do not hide real concerns
- document tradeoffs honestly
- prioritize user safety and platform reliability over winning arguments

Unacceptable behavior:

- harassment or intimidation
- personal attacks
- repeated bad-faith disruption
- unsafe guidance presented as certainty

## Enforcement

Maintainers may moderate discussions, close threads, reject contributions, or remove access when behavior harms the project or its community.

Use [SUPPORT](SUPPORT.md) and [SECURITY](SECURITY.md) for the right escalation path.
